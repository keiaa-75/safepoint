About SafePoint

Know more about the project and its mission.

SafePoint is a simple platform designed to help students report incidents of bullying and seek support. Our mission is to create a safer school environment for everyone.

This project is open source and licensed under the Mozilla Public License 2.0 and Creative Commons BY-NC-SA 4.0.

How can we do better?

Your feedback helps us improve SafePoint and create a safer, more supportive environment for all students. We value your thoughts and suggestions.
